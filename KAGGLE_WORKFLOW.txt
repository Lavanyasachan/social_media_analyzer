================================================================================
                     QUICK TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

    Problem: "Dataset not found"
    Solution: Make sure you clicked "Add Data" and added the dataset

    Problem: Out of memory
    Solution: In script, change BATCH_SIZE from 32 to 16 or 8

    Problem: Too slow
    Solution: Enable GPU in Settings → Accelerator → GPU

    Problem: Want to test quickly
    Solution: In script, change EPOCHS from 50 to 10
